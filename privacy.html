Privacy Policy

How we handle information on this site

Overview

The Blue Dispatch is committed to transparency about how we collect and use information. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve user experience and for analytics purposes. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve content and user experience. These services may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

Advertising

This site uses Ezoic to display advertisements. Ezoic may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ezoic Ad Settings.

Email Collection

We use Formspree to collect email addresses from users who sign up to receive updates. Email addresses are used solely to send newsletter updates and site announcements. We do not sell or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes. You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

This site may link to external websites or use third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at @blue_dispatch or via email at contact@thebluedispatch.co.uk.
