How The Blue Dispatch Works

This is the simple operating system behind the site: how posts are written, how they’re published, and how support works.

Thesis

Politics improves when arguments are clear, structured, and honest about trade-offs — and this site is designed to do that.

1) The format

Each dispatch is short and consistent: one thesis, a few supporting points, and a takeaway. It’s meant to be readable in a few minutes.

2) How publishing works (GitHub)

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Every post is its own HTML file in the /posts folder. When you commit a new file, it goes live automatically.

3) Frequency

The aim is consistency: a steady flow of sensible writing beats one huge essay and silence.

4) Support

Donations are optional. If someone wants to support hosting and research materials, they can do so via Revolut.
